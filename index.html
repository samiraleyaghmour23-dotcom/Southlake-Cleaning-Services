Our History

Est. 2009

Alamo City Barber College was established in the Fall of 2009. The idea was born from a conversation between Mr. Hondree Nance and a local investor in 2007 — identifying a clear need for a quality barber college in the San Antonio metropolitan area.

After demonstrating unwavering commitment to the vision, ACBC grew into a fully accredited institution serving both aspiring barbers and the local community with affordable, professional-quality services.

Today, ACBC stands as one of San Antonio's trusted barber colleges, accepting GI Bill benefits, Title IV federal financial aid, and welcoming students from all walks of life.

Funding Your Education

Financial Aid Options

We believe cost should never be a barrier to education. ACBC works with multiple funding programs to help you get started.
